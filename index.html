MOUNTAIN CYCLING

Riding mountain bikes competitively does more for a person than just making them look silly in spandex and chuncky helmets. Riding hard pushes the limits of previous achievments. Let's you know what you're capable of. Where your weaknesses are, your strengths, and your opportunities for greatness. It gives you a community. Globally. You give back, locally.

Volunteer with your local Trips for Kids chapter to connect at-risk youth with the natural world and show them the joys of mountain biking and living a healthy lifestyle. You can also visit the International Mountain Bike Associations (IMBA) website to volunteer your time either fighting for mountain bike advocacy or even getting your hands dirty with local trail building and maintenance.

Want to give mountain biking a try yourself?

Enable location through the browser, choose the difficulty you're looking to ride and the distance from your location and click the button!
